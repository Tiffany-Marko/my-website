Adventures in Archery

Hello! I'm Tiffany; I love all things archery. I personally shoot compound bows and I have a few. I love competing and just shooting. I shoot paper and foam targets. Archery was developed in Africa during the stone age and has been a classical form of weaponry and (in my case) recreation. Archery for me is a form of discipline and adventure.
